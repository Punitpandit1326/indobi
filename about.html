Our Industrial Solution

CrossBorder Limited is a leading player in the commodity trading industry, specializing in the supply and management of essential raw materials such as chrome, coal, aluminium, and other minerals. With a deep understanding of market dynamics and a commitment to sustainable practices, we ensure timely delivery and reliable supply chain solutions for industries around the world.

Our expertise spans over two decades, particularly in the African mineral markets, where we collaborate closely with suppliers and clients to create long-term value and growth opportunities. Whether you need raw materials for manufacturing, energy production, or infrastructure development, CrossBorder Limited stands as your trusted partner.

Vision

At Crossborder Trade Limited, our vision is to become India foremost commodity specialist firm, dedicated to supplying and supporting international clients with a vast network of the most reliable suppliers. We are committed to building the most diverse and extensive supplier list in the world, providing unparalleled access to the broadest, most dependable, and largest supply of hard commodities, precious metals, and petroleum products. Our mission is to meet the unique needs of every buyer by managing the entire transaction and sourcing process, regardless of scale or contract duration. With a steadfast commitment to overcoming challenges, we strive for a near 100% success rate, ensuring that our clients receive exceptional service and profitable outcomes

As we continue to grow, Crossborder Trade Limited is poised to set new standards of professionalism and efficiency, solidifying our position as a trusted partner in the global commodity market.
